Strategy Provider features

Strategy providers can connect trading accounts, upload previous trade histories, and share their trades with followers on your platform. They have access to reports that show follower counts, copied volume data, P/L on follower accounts, and commission earned. All rewards are paid to an internal wallet and then can be withdrawn to a trading account at a brokerage company or to external payment systems (such as a bank, Skrill, PayPal, and any other).

    Connect trading accounts by providing the account number and investor's password.
